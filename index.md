### Hi, I'm Youngwoo (Ray) Jeong

I'm a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at [New York University](https://engineering.nyu.edu/), advised by Professor [Austin Rovinski](https://austinrovin.ski/). My research interests include **Computer Architecture**, **Domain-Specific Accelerators**, **HW/SW Co-Design**, and **FPGA prototyping**.

Previously, I worked as a System Architect at [MangoBoost](https://www.mangoboost.io/), a DPU startup. Both my M.S. and B.S. in Electronic Engineering were earned at [Seoul National University of Science and Technology](https://soc.seoultech.ac.kr/), where I was advised by Professor [Seung Eun Lee](https://soc.seoultech.ac.kr/Professor/Professor.html) in the [Computer Architecture Lab](https://soc.seoultech.ac.kr/).
